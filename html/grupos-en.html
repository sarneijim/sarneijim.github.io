Menu

A Different, Delicious Weekend Plan You'll Remember with Friends or Family!

At Feria83, we offer our customers two fixed menus, one for brunch and another for lunch. Don't worry if you prefer sweet or savory; we have dishes to suit all tastes. Just come with a desire to enjoy and relax, and our small family will take care of the rest. What are you waiting for?

Brunch Menu

1st: A variety of toasted bread, from white sourdough to 100% whole grain with avocado, D.O. Guijuelo ham, cheese, turkey, organic scrambled eggs, butter, and homemade jams.

2nd: A variety of homemade pancakes with berries, Nutella, bacon, and maple syrup.

3rd: Mimosas

Includes one coffee per person, choose from our fantastic cappuccinos, latte macchiatos, or something more classic if you prefer.

€24

Lunch Menu

1st: Nachos to share (one serving per 4 people) and a cheese board with hummus and vegetable crudités.

2nd: Choose one of the following dishes:

  1. Vegan Stuffed Pumpkin with Couscous
  2. Chicken the Best (Marinated chicken breast with guacamole, onion, and tomato) served with french fries.
  3. Better Together Burger (Burger with bacon, brie cheese, and special sauce) served with french fries.
  4. Pulled Pork Sandwich (Brioche bread with pulled pork and apple salad served with sweet potato chips).

3rd: Coffee and a variety of our delicious homemade cakes, including the cheesecake and Oreo, chocolate, or the classic Carrot Cake.

€25
